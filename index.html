In short

We are newly established band of the small village of
Bošnjaci. Our guidline is making money and to cherish Slavonian tradition and tradition on Eastern Croatia.

Location

Our loaction is a small village in Slavonia (Eastern Croatia) called Bošnjaci. Bošnjaci has an extremely rich cultural and historical heritage dating back to the prehistoric period. Intangible cultural heritage consists primarily of the Šokačke tradition with rich folklore-traditional and ethnological value.

Goals

As a band our main goal is to amuse all people who pay to us. We are tambura players so we must earn a lot of money to make our lifes easygoing. With our music and instruments me need to bring people the memory of times that are behind us and won't happen ever again.
